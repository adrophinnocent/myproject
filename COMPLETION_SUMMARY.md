# Twina Safaris: Completion Summary

A summary of the total upgrade performed on the platform.

## Project Vision
To transform Twina Safaris into a world-class digital booking platform for Tanzanian tourism.

## Achievements
1.  **Architecture:** Upgraded to Laravel 13 for long-term support.
2.  **Design:** Reverted to the original Green & White brand aesthetic as requested.
3.  **Communication:** Built an internal Notification Center to manage leads without missing a single guest.
4.  **Utility:** Added PDF generation to provide travelers with professional trip documents immediately.

**The system is now robust, secure, and matches the classic brand identity.**
