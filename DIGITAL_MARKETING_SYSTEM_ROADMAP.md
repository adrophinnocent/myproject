# Twina Safaris: Digital Marketing Roadmap

A strategic path to scaling your online presence to reach international travelers.

## Phase 1: Local Dominance (Months 1-3)
*   Fill all Tour metadata (SEO Titles and Descriptions).
*   Create 5 high-quality blog posts about "Best time to visit Tanzania".
*   Link all Social Media handles (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube).

## Phase 2: Engagement & Trust (Months 4-6)
*   Start the Email Newsletter campaign using the built-in Subscriber system.
*   Integrate Google Reviews (See GOOGLE_REVIEWS_SETUP.md).
*   Run targeted Facebook Ads to the "Honeymoon Safaris" category.

## Phase 3: Global Expansion (Months 6+)
*   Multi-language content refinement (Ensure FR, DE, and ES translations are perfect).
*   Enable automated PDF Itineraries for all bookings to provide professional luxury service.
