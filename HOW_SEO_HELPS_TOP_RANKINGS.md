# How SEO Helps Your Rankings

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of making your website more "visible" to Google. 

## The Benefits:
1.  **Trust:** Travelers trust the top 3 results on Google more than paid ads.
2.  **Zero Cost:** Unlike Facebook Ads, organic clicks from Google are free.
3.  **Global Reach:** People in the USA or UK searching for "Mount Kilimanjaro" will find Twina Safaris directly.

## Implementation in Twina Safaris:
We have built a dedicated **SEO Tab** in your Admin Panel for every Tour and Blog post. By filling this out, you are telling Google exactly what each page is about.
