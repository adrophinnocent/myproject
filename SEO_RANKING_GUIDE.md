# SEO Ranking Guide for Twina Safaris

Follow these rules to rank on the first page of Google for terms like "Best Safari in Tanzania".

## 1. Keywords to Target
*   "Luxury Safari Tanzania"
*   "Kilimanjaro Machame Route Trek"
*   "Zanzibar Cultural Tours"
*   "Twina Safaris Moshi"

## 2. On-Page SEO Checklist
*   **Unique Titles:** Every tour must have a unique Meta Title in the SEO tab.
*   **Image Alt Tags:** Every image uploaded must have a description for Google Image search.
*   **Content Richness:** Tour descriptions should be at least 300 words long.

## 3. Technical SEO
*   **Sitemap:** The sitemap is located at `/sitemap.xml`. Submit this link to Google Search Console.
*   **Page Speed:** Keep uploaded images under 400KB to ensure fast loading on mobile.
